# Bug Report: Login Button Does Not Respond

## Bug ID
BUG-001

---

## Title
Login button does not respond after entering valid credentials.

---

## Environment
- Browser: Chrome
- OS: Windows 11

---

## Preconditions
- User is on the login page
- Valid account exists

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the login page
2. Enter a valid email
3. Enter a valid password
4. Click the "Login" button

---

## Expected Result
User should be logged into the system and redirected to the dashboard.

---

## Actual Result
Nothing happens after clicking the "Login" button.

---

## Severity
High

---

## Priority
High

---

## Status
Open
