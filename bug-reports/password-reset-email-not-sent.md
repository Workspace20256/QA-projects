# Bug Report: Password Reset Email Is Not Sent

## Bug ID
BUG-002

---

## Title
Password reset email is not sent after submitting a valid email address.

---

## Environment
- Browser: Chrome 136
- OS: Windows 11

---

## Preconditions
- User has a registered account
- User is on the password reset page

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the password reset page
2. Enter a registered email address
3. Click the "Reset Password" button

---

## Expected Result
The system should send a password reset email to the user.

---

## Actual Result
No email is received after submitting the request.

---

## Severity
High

---

## Priority
High

---

## Status
Open
